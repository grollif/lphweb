Services we offer:  Training and Lessons


There are 4 levels of training (full, partial, quarter, and per ride), and private lessons.  If it's just wanting to have your companion here to ride or being competitive in the show ring we can do it all here!  

We enjoy all ages and all breeds.  Everyone is always welcome, and as long as you want to learn I will teach you... the sky is your only limit!

Training: Full training: 5 days a week riding
Partial training: 3 days a week riding
 Quarter training: 2 days a week riding
Per ride training:  Owner pays per ride

Lessons: We offer private lessons.  If you pruchase 6 you receive the 7th free!  We can teach English and Western disciplines, we can prepare you to show at all levels, improve on your riding skills, help with fears, or start from the beginning! 

We accept: Cash, Check, & Paypal accepted.
