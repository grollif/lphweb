Welcome!


We are located in Shiocton Wisconsin, 15 minutes north of Appleton Wisconsin at McNeish Barns and Stables facility.  I have experience in working with most breeds.  I like to focus on the stock breeds such as the quarter horses, paints, appaloosas, etc., but am very willing to work with most breeds.  What ever your passion is with your horse there is a good chance I can help you achieve whatever your goals might be.  Disciplines that I train are hunt seat, western pleasure, equitation, horsemanship, showmanship, hunter hack, low fences jumping, ground work, and longe line starting.  I will also start young horses.  My goal is to get horse and owner on the same page so that when the horse goes home the owner will have confidnence in continuing the communication between owner and horse.
